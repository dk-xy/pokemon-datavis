Analysis of competitive tiers

A look into types and statistics

Explanations

Tiers are used to classify pokémons based on their power and their usage rate in battles. As cited by Smogon:

"The standards set by Smogon's tier system seek to balance competitive battling, ensuring no Pokemon is "too powerful" or over-centralizes the metagame it appears in."

In this page, you'll find a breakdown of each tier by types, with the 10 most used pokémon in each tier.
